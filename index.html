Welcome to the University of Calgary's Problem Solving Club.

If you enjoy programming, solving problems, and want to practice coding faster, more efficiently, and with fewer bugs, this is the place to be! The PSC meets every Wednesday night from 6:00-7:30pm in MS 160 for coaching and practice. Saturday practice contests are also held regularly.
