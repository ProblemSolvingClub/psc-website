Welcome

Welcome to the University of Calgary Problem Solving Club website. If you enjoy programming, solving problems, and want to practice coding faster, more efficiently, and with fewer bugs, this is the place to be! We meet every Wednesday night from 6:00-7:30pm in MS 160 for coaching and practice. Saturday practice contests are also held regularly.

2015 ACM ICPC World Finals in Morocco

Team UCalgary 3 competing in the World Finals. There, they were given 5 hours to solve 10 programming problems in C, C++, or Java. Their solutions must take test data as input and then output the correct answers for every test case. The team that solved the most problems with the least amount of time penalties wins.
