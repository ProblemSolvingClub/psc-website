About PSC

The Problem Solving Club is a group of students, faculty, and alumni that recruits and trains students to represent the University of Calgary at the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest


We strive to provide oppurtunities for students to supplement the information learned in the classroom with out of classroom experiences through regular meetings, competitions, and workshops
