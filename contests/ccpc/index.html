Calgary Collegiate Programming Contest

The Club organizes the Calgary Collegiate Programming Contest (CCPC) every winter semester. The goal of the contest if to have fun and raise interest in programming contests. Students attending a post-secondary educational institution in Calgary are invited to compete. We also welcome offsite competitors for fun and practice.
