Past Winners

The Competitive Programming Club hosts two main competitions per year, the Calgary Collegiate Programming Competition (CCPC) and the Alberta Collegiate Programming Competition (ACPC). You can find a list of all the past winners of the competitions for the last 5 years below.

The CCPC and ACPC are not only tests of technical ability but also opportunities for participants to engage with a thriving community of coders. Through intense problem-solving sessions and time-limited challenges, competitors develop skills that go beyond the classroom, preparing them for careers in software development, data science, and more. Each year brings fresh talent and new strategies, with teams constantly raising the bar for what it takes to win.

These events challenge students to push their problem-solving skills, teamwork, and coding abilities to the limit. Below, you'll find a list of all the brilliant teams and individuals who have claimed victory over the past five years.
